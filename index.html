Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Rosa and her team are researching different bacteria that could synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Rosa has a hypothesis that the microorganisms in the belly button may provide the next best source of bacteria to make synthetic beef. She has sampled the navels of hundreds of participants across the country and anonymized the samples to Test Subject ID numbers. This dashboard shows the sample data that we have retrieved from each participant. The filter can focus on the individual data from each partitipant using the Test Subject ID number. When a test subject number is selected, the panel that contains the demographic information and the charts will change to show the respective data of the selected subject number. Use the filter to get a better sense of the data that Rose and her team have collected so far.
